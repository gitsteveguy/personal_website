    About Me

    Having a keen interest in technology, especially in the domain of artificial intelligence,automation and loT, I have been recognized by notable personalities including Hon.MP & Chairman of the Parliamentary Standing Committee on IT - Dr.Shashi Tharoor, Hon Ex-MoS for the Ministry of Electronics and IT - Mr.Rajeev Chandrashekar, Hon.MoS Petroleum, Natural Gas and Tourism Mr.Suresh Gopi and Hon.MP Mr.Hybi Eden.
